How to cook Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breasts: 2, cubed
  • Frozen Vegetables: 1 bag, stir fry blend
  • Soy Sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Honey: 1 1/2 tablespoons
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, grated

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl add Soy sauce, honey, garlic, whisk to combine, and set aside.
  2. In a pan on medium-high heat, warm a drizzle of olive oil, add chicken, season with salt and pepper and then cook for 4-5 minutes until mostly cooked. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add frozen vegetables, season with salt and pepper and then cook until tender.
  4. Return chicken to the pan, add the honey garlic sauce, and cook for a final 3-4 minutes until sauce thickens and coats on chicken and vegetables.
